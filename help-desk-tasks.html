🛠️ Common Help Desk Tasks

Tag: Help Desk

Everyday IT support tasks made simple.

Steps

# What to do How to do it
1 Reset a password Go to your company portal or call IT. For Windows login, press Ctrl+Alt+Delete > Change a Password.
2 Account locked out Wait 15 minutes and try again, or contact IT to unlock your account.
3 Computer running slow Restart your computer first. Close programs you are not using. Check Task Manager (Ctrl+Shift+Esc) for what is using the most resources.
4 Can't connect to internet Unplug your router/modem, wait 30 seconds, plug back in. If on Wi-Fi, forget the network and reconnect.
5 Software won't open Right-click the program and Run as Administrator. If it still won't open, restart your computer.
6 Request new software Contact IT with the name of the program you need and your business reason for needing it.
7 Report an issue to IT Write down what happened, any error messages, and what you were doing. The more detail the better.
