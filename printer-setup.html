🖨️ Printer Setup & Troubleshooting

Tag: Printers

Add a printer and fix common print problems.

Steps

# What to do How to do it
1 Add a local printer Go to Settings > Bluetooth & devices > Printers & scanners > Add device.
2 Add a network printer Open File Explorer, type \\printserver in the address bar. Double-click your printer to install it.
3 Print queue stuck Go to Settings > Printers, click your printer, open the print queue, right-click stuck jobs and click Cancel.
4 Wrong printer prints job Before printing, always check the printer name shown in the print dialog. Change it if needed.
5 Update printer driver Go to Device Manager > Printers > right-click your printer > Update Driver > Search Automatically.
6 Remove old printers Go to Settings > Bluetooth & devices > Printers & scanners, click a printer you no longer use, and click Remove Device.
