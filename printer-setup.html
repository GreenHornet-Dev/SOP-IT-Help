🖨️ Printer Setup & Troubleshooting

Printers

Add a printer and fix common print problems.

Steps

#What to DoHow to Do It
1Add a local printerGo to Settings > Bluetooth & devices > Printers & scanners > Add device.
2Add a network printerOpen File Explorer, type \\printserver in the address bar. Double-click your printer to install it.
3Print queue stuckGo to Settings > Printers, click your printer, open the print queue, right-click stuck jobs and click Cancel.
4Wrong printer prints jobBefore printing, always check the printer name shown in the print dialog. Change it if needed.
5Update printer driverGo to Device Manager > Printers > right-click your printer > Update Driver > Search Automatically.
6Remove old printersGo to Settings > Bluetooth & devices > Printers & scanners, click a printer you no longer use, and click Remove Device.
