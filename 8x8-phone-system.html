📞 8x8 Phone System

8x8

Set up and use your 8x8 business phone system.

Setup & Usage

#What to DoHow to Do It
1Download 8x8 WorkGo to 8x8.com/downloads or your company's software portal and install the 8x8 Work app.
2Sign in to 8x8Open 8x8 Work and sign in with your company credentials.
3Make a callClick the dialpad icon, enter the phone number, and click the green call button.
4Transfer a callDuring a call, click Transfer. Dial the number to transfer to, then click Complete Transfer.
5Set up voicemailGo to Settings > Voicemail. Follow the prompts to record your greeting.
6Fix no dial toneCheck your internet connection. Close and reopen the app. If still no audio, check your microphone/speaker settings.
7Forward callsGo to Settings > Call Forwarding. Enter the number to forward to and toggle it on.
