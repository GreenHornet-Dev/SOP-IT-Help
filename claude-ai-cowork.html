🟢 Claude AI & CoWork SOP

AI Tools

Use Claude AI for IT help and CoWork for company-specific policies and documents.

Using Claude AI

#What to DoHow to Do It
1Open Claude AIGo to claude.ai in your browser and sign in with your work account.
2Ask a questionType your question in plain English. Example: How do I reset my Outlook password?
3Refine the answerIf the answer is not quite right, type Can you simplify that? or Give me step-by-step instructions.

Using CoWork

#What to DoHow to Do It
1Open CoWorkGo to your CoWork link or find it in your browser bookmarks. Sign in with your work account.
2Use CoWork for company tasksType what you need help with. CoWork can answer questions about your company's own policies and documents.

AI Privacy & Usage Guidelines

#RuleDetail
1Never enter sensitive dataDo not type passwords, SSNs, or private client data into any AI tool.
2Verify important answersAlways double-check critical answers. AI is a helper, not a replacement for your IT team.
3Follow company AI policyUse Claude and CoWork only for approved work tasks as outlined in company policy.
