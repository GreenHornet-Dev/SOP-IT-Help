🟢 Claude AI & CoWork SOP

AI Tools

Use AI tools to get help faster.

Steps

#What to DoHow to Do It
1Open Claude AIGo to claude.ai in your browser and sign in with your work account.
2Ask a questionType your question in plain English. Example: How do I reset my Outlook password?
3Get a better answerIf the answer is not quite right, type "Can you simplify that?" or "Give me step-by-step instructions."
4Open CoWorkGo to your CoWork link or find it in your browser bookmarks. Sign in with your work account.
5Use CoWork for tasksType what you need help with. CoWork can answer questions about your company's own policies and documents.
6Privacy reminderNever type passwords, SSNs, or private client data into any AI tool.
7AI can be wrongAlways double-check important answers. AI is a helper, not a replacement for your IT team.
