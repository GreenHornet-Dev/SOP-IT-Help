💬 Microsoft Teams

Teams

Set up Teams and fix common issues.

Steps

#What to DoHow to Do It
1Install TeamsDownload from teams.microsoft.com or find it in your company's software portal.
2Sign in to TeamsOpen Teams and sign in with your work Microsoft 365 account.
3Join a meetingClick the meeting link in your calendar or email. Click Join now.
4Fix no audio in meetingClick the three dots > Device settings. Make sure the correct microphone and speaker are selected.
5Share your screenIn a meeting, click Share > Screen or Window. Select what you want others to see.
6Teams not loadingClose Teams completely from the system tray, reopen it. If still failing, clear cache: go to %appdata%\Microsoft\Teams and delete the Cache folder.
7Set statusClick your profile picture > Set status message or change your status dot.
