💬 Microsoft Teams

Teams

Install, use, and troubleshoot Microsoft Teams for meetings, chat, and screen sharing.

Install & Sign In

#What to DoHow to Do It
1Install TeamsDownload from teams.microsoft.com or find it in your company's software portal.
2Sign in to TeamsOpen Teams and sign in with your work Microsoft 365 account.
3Set your statusClick your profile picture > Set status message or change your status dot.

Meetings & Screen Share

#What to DoHow to Do It
1Join a meetingClick the meeting link in your calendar or email, then click Join now.
2Fix no audio in meetingClick the three dots > Device settings. Make sure the correct microphone and speaker are selected.
3Share your screenIn a meeting, click Share > Screen or Window. Select what you want others to see.

Troubleshooting

#ProblemFix
1Teams not loadingClose Teams completely from the system tray, then reopen. If still failing, clear the cache (see Quick Reference below).
2Teams crashes on launchRun the Clear Cache one-liner, then restart Teams.
3Missing meetings or chatsSign out of Teams (profile > Sign out), then sign back in to force a full sync.

Quick Reference — One-Liners

TaskCommand (PowerShell)
Kill TeamsStop-Process -Name Teams -Force -ErrorAction SilentlyContinue
Clear Teams cacheRemove-Item -Path "$env:APPDATA\Microsoft\Teams\Cache" -Recurse -Force -ErrorAction SilentlyContinue
Clear full Teams app dataGet-ChildItem "$env:APPDATA\Microsoft\Teams" -Exclude "desktop-config.json","settings.json" | Remove-Item -Recurse -Force -ErrorAction SilentlyContinue
Restart TeamsStart-Process "$env:LOCALAPPDATA\Microsoft\Teams\Update.exe" "--processStart" "Teams.exe"
Check Teams version(Get-AppxPackage -Name "MicrosoftTeams").Version
