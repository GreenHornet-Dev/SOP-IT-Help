💬 Microsoft Teams

Tag: Teams

Set up Teams and fix common issues.

Steps

# What to do How to do it
1 Install Teams Download from teams.microsoft.com or find it in your company's software portal.
2 Sign in to Teams Open Teams and sign in with your work Microsoft 365 account.
3 Join a meeting Click the meeting link in your calendar or email. Click Join now.
4 Fix no audio in meeting Click the three dots > Device settings. Make sure the correct microphone and speaker are selected.
5 Share your screen In a meeting, click Share > Screen or Window. Select what you want others to see.
6 Fix Teams not loading Close Teams completely from the system tray, reopen it. If still failing, clear cache: go to %appdata%\Microsoft\Teams and delete the Cache folder.
7 Set status Click your profile picture > Set status message or change your status dot.
