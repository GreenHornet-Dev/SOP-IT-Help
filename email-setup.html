📧 Email / Mail Setup & Troubleshooting

Mail

Add email accounts, fix sending issues, recover deleted emails, and configure Out of Office in Outlook.

Add an Email Account

#What to DoHow to Do It
1Add email account in OutlookOpen Outlook > File > Add Account. Enter your email and follow the prompts.

Out of Office (Automatic Replies)

#What to DoHow to Do It
1Set up Out of OfficeGo to File > Automatic Replies. Set the date range and message, then click OK.
2Turn off Out of OfficeGo to File > Automatic Replies and select Do not send automatic replies.

Troubleshoot Outlook

#ProblemFix
1Emails not sendingCheck your internet connection. Then go to Outbox and try resending. If stuck, restart Outlook.
2Recover deleted emailsGo to the Deleted Items folder. Right-click email > Restore.
3Outlook not openingClose Outlook, open Task Manager and end any Outlook processes, then reopen. Or use the one-liner below.
4Outlook running slow or freezingClear the .ost cache (see Quick Reference). Back up the file first if possible.

Quick Reference — One-Liners

TaskCommand (PowerShell)
Kill OutlookStop-Process -Name OUTLOOK -Force -ErrorAction SilentlyContinue
Restart OutlookStop-Process -Name OUTLOOK -Force -ErrorAction SilentlyContinue; Start-Process OUTLOOK
List .ost cache filesGet-ChildItem "$env:LOCALAPPDATA\Microsoft\Outlook" -Filter "*.ost" | Select-Object Name, Length, LastWriteTime
Open Outlook safe modeStart-Process "OUTLOOK" "/safe"
Check mail profileStart-Process "control" "mlcfg32.cpl"
