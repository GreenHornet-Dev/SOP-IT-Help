📧 Email / Mail Setup & Troubleshooting

Mail

Set up and fix email problems.

Steps

#What to DoHow to Do It
1Add email account in OutlookOpen Outlook > File > Add Account. Enter your email and follow the prompts.
2Fix emails not sendingCheck your internet connection. Then go to Outbox and try resending. If stuck, restart Outlook.
3Recover deleted emailsGo to the Deleted Items folder. Right-click email > Restore.
4Set up Out of OfficeFile > Automatic Replies. Set the date range and message.
5Fix Outlook not openingClose Outlook, open Task Manager and end any Outlook processes, then reopen.
