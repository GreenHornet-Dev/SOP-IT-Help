💼 Office Apps

Office 365

Install, fix, and use Microsoft 365 apps.

Steps

#What to DoHow to Do It
1Install Microsoft 365Go to office.com, sign in, click Install Office > Office 365 apps. Run the installer.
2Activate OfficeOpen any Office app. If it asks for activation, sign in with your work Microsoft account.
3Office app crashesClose the app, open Start > Settings > Apps, find the app, click Modify > Quick Repair.
4Update Office appsOpen any Office app > File > Account > Update Options > Update Now.
5Save to OneDriveClick File > Save As > OneDrive. Choose your work folder and click Save.
6Open recent filesClick File > Recent in any Office app to see recently opened files.
