💼 Office Apps

Office 365

Install, activate, repair, and update Microsoft 365 Office applications.

Install & Activate

#What to DoHow to Do It
1Install Microsoft 365Go to office.com, sign in, click Install Office > Office 365 apps. Run the installer.
2Activate OfficeOpen any Office app. If it asks for activation, sign in with your work Microsoft account.

Repair & Update

#What to DoHow to Do It
1Office app crashesClose the app, open Start > Settings > Apps, find the Office app, click Modify > Quick Repair.
2Update Office appsOpen any Office app > File > Account > Update Options > Update Now.
3Online repair (deeper fix)Go to Settings > Apps > Microsoft 365 > Modify > Online Repair. Requires internet; fixes most persistent issues.

Files & Storage

#What to DoHow to Do It
1Save to OneDriveClick File > Save As > OneDrive. Choose your work folder and click Save.
2Open recent filesClick File > Recent in any Office app to see recently opened files.
