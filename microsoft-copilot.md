# ✨ Microsoft Copilot

**Tag:** Copilot

Use Copilot built into Microsoft 365 apps.

## Steps

| # | What to do | How to do it |
|---|------------|--------------|
| 1 | Open Copilot in Word | Open a Word document. Click the Copilot icon in the ribbon or press Alt+I. |
| 2 | Summarize a document | In Word or Outlook, click Copilot > Summarize. It will give you a short summary. |
| 3 | Draft an email | In Outlook, click New Email > Copilot > Draft with Copilot. Describe what you want and click Generate. |
| 4 | Use Copilot in Teams | In a Teams chat or meeting, click the Copilot icon to summarize or ask questions about the conversation. |
| 5 | Use Copilot in Excel | Select data, then click Copilot in the ribbon. Ask it to analyze, chart, or explain your data. |
| 6 | Copilot privacy | Copilot follows your company's data policies. Do not enter confidential or personal data. |
