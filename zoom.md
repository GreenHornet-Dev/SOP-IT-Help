# 📹 Zoom

**Tag:** Zoom

Join and host Zoom meetings.

## Steps

| # | What to do | How to do it |
|---|------------|--------------|
| 1 | Download Zoom | Go to zoom.us/download and download Zoom Desktop Client. Run the installer. |
| 2 | Join a meeting | Click the meeting link from your email or calendar. Click Open Zoom > Join with Computer Audio. |
| 3 | Mute/unmute yourself | Press the spacebar to temporarily unmute, or click the Mute button in the toolbar. |
| 4 | Share your screen | Click Share Screen in the toolbar. Choose the window or screen you want to share. |
| 5 | Use the waiting room | Hosts: click Participants > Admit to let people in from the waiting room. |
| 6 | Record a meeting | Click Record in the toolbar. Choose Record to Computer or Record to Cloud. |
| 7 | Fix audio issues | Click the up arrow next to Mute > Audio settings. Test your speaker and microphone. |
