🔐 Okta

Identity

Manage identity and access through Okta.

Steps

#What to DoHow to Do It
1Log in to OktaGo to your company's Okta URL (e.g. company.okta.com). Enter your email and password.
2Set up MFAAfter logging in, go to Settings > Extra Verification. Add Okta Verify or SMS as a second factor.
3Reset passwordOn the Okta login page click Forgot password. Enter your email and follow the reset steps.
4Unlock accountContact IT. An admin can unlock the account in the Okta Admin Console under Directory > People.
5Assign app to userIn Okta Admin Console: Applications > find the app > Assignments tab > Assign to People.
6Change UPN/usernameIn Okta Admin Console: Directory > People > find user > Profile > Edit the Login field.
