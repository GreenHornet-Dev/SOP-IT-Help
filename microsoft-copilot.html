🤖 Microsoft Copilot

Copilot

Use Copilot built into Microsoft 365 apps.

Steps

#What to DoHow to Do It
1Open Copilot in WordOpen a Word document. Click the Copilot icon in the ribbon or press Alt+I.
2Summarize a documentIn Word or Outlook, click Copilot > Summarize. It will give you a short summary.
3Draft an emailIn Outlook, click New Email > Copilot > Draft with Copilot. Describe what you want and click Generate.
4Use Copilot in TeamsIn a Teams chat or meeting, click the Copilot icon to summarize or ask questions about the conversation.
5Use Copilot in ExcelSelect data, then click Copilot in the ribbon. Ask it to analyze, chart, or explain your data.
6Copilot privacyCopilot follows your company's data policies. Do not enter confidential or personal data.
