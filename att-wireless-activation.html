📶 AT&T Wireless Activation

WIRELESS

Activate a new AT&T business wireless line from the admin portal and set up the device for the end user.

Admin Portal — Activate the Line

#What to DoHow to Do It
1Log into AT&T Business PortalGo to businessdirect.att.com and sign in with your business admin credentials.
2Navigate to Manage DevicesFrom the dashboard: My Wireless > Manage Devices & Features.
3Select line and click ActivateFind the account/line > click the phone number row > select Activate.
4Enter IMEI and SIM ICCIDEnter the IMEI (on box or Settings > About Phone) and SIM ICCID (on SIM card or packaging).
5Confirm plan and featuresVerify data plan, hotspot, add-ons > click Submit / Confirm Activation.
6Save confirmation numberNote the confirmation number and allow 5–15 minutes for activation to complete.

End User — Device Setup

#What to DoHow to Do It
1Insert SIM and rebootInsert SIM card into device (if physical) or confirm eSIM was pushed. Power off, wait 30 sec, power on.
2Check for AT&T signalLook for AT&T signal bars in the top-right corner. May take up to 15 minutes to appear.
3Test the lineMake a test call, send a text, and confirm mobile data is working.
4Set up voicemailHold 1 on the keypad or dial *86. Follow prompts to record greeting and set PIN.
5MDM enrollmentOpen Company Portal app or contact IT to enroll the device in MDM/Intune if required.

Troubleshooting

#ProblemFix
1No signal after 30 minutesCall AT&T Business Support: 1-800-331-0500. Have IMEI and order confirmation number ready.
2eSIM not pushed to deviceCheck the admin portal to confirm eSIM provisioning completed. Re-push from the portal if needed.
3Calls not working but data isConfirm VoLTE is enabled on the device: Settings > Cellular > Enable LTE > Voice & Data.
