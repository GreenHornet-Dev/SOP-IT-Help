📱 AT&T Wireless Activation

Tag: WIRELESS

Activate new AT&T wireless lines via Business Portal and guide end users through device setup.

Steps

# What to do How to do it
1 Log into AT&T Business Portal Go to businessdirect.att.com and sign in with your business admin credentials.
2 Navigate to Manage Devices From dashboard: My Wireless > Manage Devices & Features.
3 Select line and click Activate Find the account/line > click the phone number row > select Activate.
4 Enter IMEI and SIM ICCID Enter the IMEI (on box or Settings > About Phone) and SIM ICCID (on SIM card or packaging).
5 Confirm plan and features Verify data plan, hotspot, add-ons > click Submit / Confirm Activation.
6 Save confirmation number Note the confirmation number and allow 5-15 minutes for activation to complete.
7 END USER - Insert SIM & reboot Insert SIM card into device (if physical) or confirm eSIM was pushed. Power off, wait 30 sec, power on.
8 END USER - Check for AT&T signal Look for AT&T signal bars in top right corner. May take up to 15 minutes to appear.
9 END USER - Test the line Make a test call, send a text, and confirm mobile data is working.
10 END USER - Set up voicemail Hold 1 on the keypad or dial *86. Follow prompts to record greeting and set PIN.
11 END USER - MDM enrollment Open Company Portal app or contact IT to enroll device in MDM/Intune if required.
12 No signal after 30 min? Call AT&T Business Support: 1-800-331-0500. Have IMEI and order confirmation number ready.
