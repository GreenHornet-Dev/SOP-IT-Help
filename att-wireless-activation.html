AT&T Wireless Activation

WIRELESS

Activate new AT&T wireless lines via Business Portal and guide end users through device setup.

Steps

#What to DoHow to Do It
1Log into AT&T Business PortalGo to businessdirect.att.com and sign in with your business admin credentials.
2Navigate to Manage DevicesFrom dashboard: My Wireless > Manage Devices & Features.
3Select line and click ActivateFind the account/line > click the phone number row > select Activate.
4Enter IMEI and SIM ICCIDEnter the IMEI (on box or Settings > About Phone) and SIM ICCID (on SIM card or packaging).
5Confirm plan and featuresVerify data plan, hotspot, add-ons > click Submit / Confirm Activation.
6Save confirmation numberNote the confirmation number and allow 5-15 minutes for activation to complete.
7END USER - Insert SIM and rebootInsert SIM card into device (if physical) or confirm eSIM was pushed. Power off, wait 30 sec, power on.
8END USER - Check for AT&T signalLook for AT&T signal bars in top right corner. May take up to 15 minutes to appear.
9END USER - Test the lineMake a test call, send a text, and confirm mobile data is working.
10END USER - Set up voicemailHold 1 on the keypad or dial *86. Follow prompts to record greeting and set PIN.
11END USER - MDM enrollmentOpen Company Portal app or contact IT to enroll device in MDM/Intune if required.
12No signal after 30 min?Call AT&T Business Support: 1-800-331-0500. Have IMEI and order confirmation number ready.
