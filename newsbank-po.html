How to Submit a Purchase Order

Step 1 — Open the PO App

Open the Purchase Order PowerApp from your SharePoint intranet or the link provided by IT. Sign in with your NewsBank Microsoft account if prompted.

Step 2 — Fill in the PO form

  • Department — auto-filled from your device record (change if ordering for a different department)
  • GL Code — auto-filled based on your department; change if needed
  • Items — add each item: description, quantity, unit price, and vendor
  • Shipping — select shipping method and enter estimated shipping cost
  • CC Recipients — optional; add other people to CC on approval emails (e.g. your department head or a co-worker receiving the order)
Common items: Headset, 32" Monitor, USB-C Charger, Adobe Creative Cloud, Gemini / Claude AI subscription. See Common Orders for descriptions and typical vendors.

Step 3 — Submit

Click Submit PO. The system will:

  • Assign a PO number
  • Create a Jira HELP ticket for IT tracking
  • Send your manager an approval request
  • Email you and the Purchasing team as confirmation the PO was submitted
Note: Submitting does not mean the order is placed. It goes to your manager for approval first.

What Happens After You Submit

PO Submitted

You receive a confirmation email. Your manager receives an approval request. Purchasing is CC'd. A Jira HELP ticket (HELP-XXXX) is created for IT tracking.

Manager Reviews

Your manager receives an email approval card. They review the PO details and click Approve or Reject with optional notes. There is no set deadline — follow up with your manager if you haven't heard back.

If Approved

You receive an approval confirmation. Purchasing receives the approved PO and will place the order. The Jira ticket is updated to show approved status with a comment from the manager.

If Rejected

You receive a rejection email with the manager's reason. The Jira ticket is updated. Review the feedback, update your PO if needed, and resubmit.

Order Placed & Received

Purchasing places the order and updates the Jira ticket. When the item arrives, IT will arrange delivery or notify you to pick it up.

Check Your PO Status

Method 1 — Check your email

You receive an email at each stage: submission, approval/rejection, and (when set up) order placed. Check your inbox for emails with subject "Purchase Order #XXXX".

Method 2 — Check the Jira ticket

Every PO has a corresponding HELP-XXXX Jira ticket. The Jira ticket link was included in your submission confirmation email. Click it to see the current status and any comments.

Jira StatusWhat it means

Method 3 — Contact IT

Email helpdesk@newsbank.com with your PO number and we'll look it up for you.

FAQ

How long does approval take?

There's no set SLA — it depends on your manager. If you need it urgently, reach out to your manager directly and reference your PO number. Most standard requests are approved within 1–2 business days.

Can I submit a PO for someone else?

Yes. Fill in the form with your own credentials (you're the requestor of record), but in the Notes/Description field clarify that the item is for a specific team member or department. You can also add their manager to the CC field if the approval chain is different.

What if I submitted with the wrong information?

If your PO hasn't been approved yet, email helpdesk@newsbank.com with your PO number and the correction. We can update the Jira ticket. If it's already been approved, Purchasing will need to be notified before the order is placed.

How do I know which GL code to use?

The PO form auto-fills your department's default GL code based on your Lansweeper device record. If you're ordering for a different department, select it from the dropdown. If you're unsure, contact your manager or Finance before submitting.

My department isn't showing in the dropdown.

The dropdown is populated from Lansweeper asset data. If your device isn't scanned by Lansweeper yet or the department field is blank, contact IT to update your device record. In the meantime, type your department name manually or select the closest match.

My manager didn't receive the approval email.

Ask your manager to check their spam/junk folder. If it's not there, email helpdesk@newsbank.com with your PO number and we'll resend the approval request.

The PO app isn't loading.

Try a hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R), clear your browser cache, or open in a different browser. If the issue persists, email helpdesk@newsbank.com.

What's the PO payment term?

Standard terms are Net 30, requiring manager approval. Purchasing handles invoicing and payment — you don't need to pay out of pocket.
