📹 Zoom

Zoom

Install, join meetings, and troubleshoot audio, video, and screen sharing in Zoom.

Install & Join a Meeting

#What to DoHow to Do It
1Install ZoomDownload from zoom.us/download. Run the installer and sign in with your work email.
2Join a meetingClick the meeting link or open Zoom, click Join, and enter the meeting ID and passcode.
3Update ZoomClick your profile picture in Zoom > Check for Updates.

Audio, Video & Screen Share

#What to DoHow to Do It
1Fix audio not workingClick the arrow next to the microphone icon and select the correct audio device. Test speaker and microphone.
2Share your screenClick Share Screen in the meeting controls. Choose the window or screen to share.
3Fix camera not showingClick the arrow next to the camera icon. Select the correct camera from the list.

Troubleshooting

#ProblemFix
1Black screen when sharingTry sharing a specific window instead of the whole screen. Disable GPU acceleration in Zoom Settings > Video > Advanced if it persists.
2Zoom crashes or freezesKill Zoom via Task Manager, clear the cache (see Quick Reference), then restart Zoom.
3Echo or feedback during callUse headphones to prevent mic feedback, or mute other participants if you are the host.

Quick Reference — One-Liners

TaskCommand (PowerShell)
Kill ZoomStop-Process -Name Zoom -Force -ErrorAction SilentlyContinue
Clear Zoom logs & cacheRemove-Item -Path "$env:APPDATA\Zoom\logs" -Recurse -Force -ErrorAction SilentlyContinue
Full Zoom data resetStop-Process -Name Zoom -Force -ErrorAction SilentlyContinue; Remove-Item -Path "$env:APPDATA\Zoom" -Recurse -Force -ErrorAction SilentlyContinue
Check Zoom install pathGet-Command zoom | Select-Object Source
