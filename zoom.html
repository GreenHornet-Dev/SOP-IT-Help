📹 Zoom

Zoom

Set up and troubleshoot Zoom meetings.

Steps

#What to DoHow to Do It
1Install ZoomDownload from zoom.us/download. Run the installer and sign in with your work email.
2Join a meetingClick the meeting link or open Zoom, click Join, and enter the meeting ID and passcode.
3Fix audio not workingClick the arrow next to the microphone icon and select the correct audio device. Test speaker and microphone.
4Share your screenClick Share Screen in the meeting controls. Choose the window or screen to share.
5Fix black screen when sharingTry sharing a specific window instead of the whole screen. Disable GPU acceleration in Zoom settings if it persists.
6Update ZoomClick your profile picture in Zoom > Check for Updates.
