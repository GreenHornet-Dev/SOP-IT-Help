# 💼 Office Apps

**Tag:** Office 365

Install, fix, and use Microsoft 365 apps.

## Steps

| # | What to do | How to do it |
|---|------------|--------------|
| 1 | Install Microsoft 365 | Go to office.com, sign in, click Install Office > Office 365 apps. Run the installer. |
| 2 | Activate Office | Open any Office app. If it asks for activation, sign in with your work Microsoft account. |
| 3 | Office app crashes | Close the app, open Start > Settings > Apps, find the app, click Modify > Quick Repair. |
| 4 | Update Office apps | Open any Office app > File > Account > Update Options > Update Now. |
| 5 | Save to OneDrive | Click File > Save As > OneDrive. Choose your work folder and click Save. |
| 6 | Open recent files | Click File > Recent in any Office app to see recently opened files. |
