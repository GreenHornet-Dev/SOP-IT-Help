# 🤖 Claude AI & CoWork SOP

**Tag:** AI Tools

Use AI tools to get help faster.

## Steps

| # | What to do | How to do it |
|---|------------|--------------|
| 1 | Open Claude AI | Go to claude.ai in your browser and sign in with your work account. |
| 2 | Ask a question | Type your question in plain English. Example: How do I reset my Outlook password? |
| 3 | Get a better answer | If the answer is not quite right, type Can you simplify that? or Give me step-by-step instructions. |
| 4 | Open CoWork | Go to your CoWork link or find it in your browser bookmarks. Sign in with your work account. |
| 5 | Use CoWork for tasks | Type what you need help with. CoWork can answer questions about your company's own policies and documents. |
| 6 | Privacy reminder | Never type passwords, SSNs, or private client data into any AI tool. |
| 7 | AI can be wrong | Always double-check important answers. AI is a helper, not a replacement for your IT team. |
