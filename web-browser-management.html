🌐 Web Browser Management

Browser

Fix and manage your web browser.

Steps

#What to DoHow to Do It
1Clear browser cacheGo to Settings > Privacy > Clear browsing data. Check Cache and Cookies. Click Clear data.
2Update the browserGo to Settings > Help > About. The browser will check for updates automatically.
3Reset browser settingsGo to Settings > Reset settings. This fixes most unusual browser behavior.
4Manage extensionsGo to Settings > Extensions. Disable or remove extensions causing issues.
5Set default browserGo to Windows Settings > Apps > Default Apps. Choose your preferred browser.
