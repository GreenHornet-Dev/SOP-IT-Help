🌐 Web Browser Management

Tag: Browser

Fix and manage your web browser.

Steps

# What to do How to do it
1 Clear browser cache Go to Settings > Privacy > Clear browsing data. Check Cache and Cookies. Click Clear data.
2 Update the browser Go to Settings > Help > About. The browser will check for updates automatically.
3 Reset browser settings Go to Settings > Reset settings. This fixes most unusual browser behavior.
4 Manage extensions Go to Settings > Extensions. Disable or remove extensions causing issues.
5 Set default browser Go to Windows Settings > Apps > Default Apps. Choose your preferred browser.
